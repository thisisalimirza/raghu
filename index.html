My Journey

Early Life & Education

Raghuram Reddy was born in Columbus, Ohio and grew up in Dallas, Texas. He graduated from the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science. He later graduated from the University of Miami with a degree in microbiology and immunology in 2020.

Research Fellowship

He then spent a year as a National Institutes of Health Intramural Research Training Award Fellow conducting primary cilia research regarding glioblastoma and Parkinson's disease.

Current Studies

He is currently a third-year medical student at Florida International University Herbert Wertheim College of Medicine.

Research & Publications

His research interests include brain tumors, medical humanities, cancer, spine, and space medicine. He has reviewed for the American Medical Association of Journal of Medical Ethics, World Neurosurgery: X, and Family Medicine.

Professional Involvement

He is actively involved in improving healthcare policy as part of the Florida Medical Association and National Brain Tumor Society.

Personal Interests

Aside from medicine, his hobbies include spending time with family and friends, trying new restaurants and coffee shops, acrylic painting, outdoor activities, and more.
